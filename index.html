Classrooms Under Fire 1999 - 2023

Introduction

The United States is known for its strong gun ownership rights.

Consequently, it ranks among the highest in the world for gun-related incidents.

This visualisation explores school shootings in the US from 1999 to 2023 to
provide perspective on the impact of these rights on students.

Design by: Yong Ying Lai         Data source: Kaggle

The 1st visualisation, combining proportional symbol map and step chart, discover the total number of student injuries across the years.

Secondly, the bar chart displays the number of students exposed to gun violence, which corresponds to the size of school enrolment involved in the shooting incidents.

Then, the shooter's profile is revealed, which includes the median age of the shooters and their intentions related to gun violence.
